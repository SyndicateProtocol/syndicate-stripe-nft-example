How it works

Earn Points

  • Stamina:1-10 points based on subscription duration.
  • Creator:1-10 points awarded for creating projects.
  • Collaborator:1-10 points awarded for joining projects as a collaborator.
  • Advisor:1-10 points awarded for commenting on projects.
  • Builder:1-10 points awarded for building and sharing plugins with the community.
  • Evangelist:1-10 points awarded for successfully inviting new members.
